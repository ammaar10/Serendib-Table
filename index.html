Our story

Rooted in Tradition,
Served with Love

Serendib Table was born from a simple belief - that food is memory. Our kitchen draws from the rich culinary heritage of Sri Lanka, from the coastal spice of Galle to the hill country flavours of Kandy.

Every dish is prepared fresh daily using traditional clay pots, hand-ground spice blends, and recipes that have never been written down - only passed on.
